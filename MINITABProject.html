Minitab Project

This is a group project in analyzing a dataset using Minitab Software under the Advanced Statistics and Analytics module in Year 2 - Semester 2.

Global Air Transport represent a crucial role in facilitating global connectivity, enabling the seamless movement of passengers and goods
across the world. This project primary goal is to give a thorough picture of the patterns in international air travel for the majority of nations
between 2005 and 2019 including in Air transport - registered carrier departures, Air transport - freight (million ton - km) , Air transport -
passengers carried and Logistics performance index etc.
